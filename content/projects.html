Programming functions to a Calculator

As a tech-savy student on an economics analysis course I was recruited by the professor to create a series of tutorials for other students to follow.
My tasks were to code the functions on a simulator for the calculator, record the voice actress and edit the video.
The videos were published by the university's logistics laboratory and have since been used every academic term.
